# Blood Cells Notes

## Overview

There are 3 main types of blood cells relevant to this project:

| Cell Type | Full Name | 
|---|---|
| RBC | Red Blood Cell | 
| WBC | White Blood Cell | 
| Platelet | Thrombocyte | 

---

## RBC — Red Blood Cell 

### Shape
- Round, biconcave disc (like a donut without a hole)
- Very uniform in size — all RBCs look almost identical
- No nucleus (this is unique — most human cells have a nucleus)

### Visual Characteristics in Images
- Appear as **pale pink / salmon colored** circles (due to hemoglobin staining)
- Have a slightly lighter center because of the biconcave shape
- Most **abundant** cell in a blood smear — they fill most of the image
- Easy to detect because of their consistent round shape and color

### Role
- Carry **oxygen** from the lungs to all parts of the body
- Carry **carbon dioxide** back to the lungs to be exhaled
- Contain hemoglobin — the protein that binds to oxygen

---

## WBC — White Blood Cell

### Shape
- Larger and more irregular than RBCs
- Have a visible **nucleus** (dark-stained, often multi-lobed or kidney-shaped)
- Several subtypes exist (neutrophil, lymphocyte, monocyte, etc.) — each looks different

### Visual Characteristics in Images
- Appear **larger and darker** than RBCs due to the stained nucleus
- Nucleus stains **dark purple / blue**
- Much **less common** than RBCs — only a few per image
- Easiest to spot because of their size and dark nucleus

### Role
- Part of the **immune system** — they defend the body against infections
- Attack bacteria, viruses, and foreign substances
- A high WBC count can signal infection or disease

---

## Platelets — Thrombocyte 

### Shape
- Very **small** — much smaller than RBCs
- Irregular, fragment-like shape (not a full cell — they are cell fragments)
- No nucleus

### Visual Characteristics in Images
- Appear as **tiny dark purple dots** scattered around the image
- Can be hard to detect because of their small size
- Often appear in clusters near damaged tissue
- The **hardest** of the 3 types to detect reliably in images

### Role
- Responsible for **blood clotting**
- When a blood vessel is damaged, platelets rush to the site and clump together to form a clot
- Without platelets, even a small cut could cause dangerous bleeding

---

## Quick Comparison

| Feature | RBC | WBC | Platelet |
|---|---|---|---|
| Size | Medium | Largest | Smallest |
| Shape | Biconcave disc | Irregular, lobed | Tiny fragment |
| Has nucleus? | No | Yes | No |
| Color in image | Pale pink | Dark purple nucleus | Tiny dark dots |
| Abundance | Most common | Rare | Scattered |
| Main role | Carry oxygen | Fight infection | Blood clotting |
| Detection difficulty | Easy | Medium | Hard |
